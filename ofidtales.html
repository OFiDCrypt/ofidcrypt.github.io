Characters

Giddy

Giddy is the fun-loving, happy-go-lucky member of the OFiD Crew, bringing joy and excitement wherever she goes. Adorned in vibrant shades of purple, pink, and teal, Giddy is a burst of color and energy. She is never seen without her beloved bouncy ball, which she carries with her on all her adventures. Giddy's infectious enthusiasm and playful nature make her the life of the party, always finding a way to make everyone smile. Whether it's cracking jokes or leading the crew in a spontaneous dance-off, Giddy, also known as "She Giddy," knows how to keep spirits high and create unforgettable memories. Her positive outlook and carefree attitude make her an indispensable member of the OFiD Crew.

Rex

Rex is the cute and shy member of the OFiD Crew, always wearing his denim vest and glasses.
