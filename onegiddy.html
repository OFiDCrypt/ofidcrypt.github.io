What is ONE GIDDY StableToken?

Canada’s first (unofficial) stable digital asset, designed to reflect the value of 1 Canadian Dollar through real-time commerce. It captures the spirit of our magical character Giddy — playful, trustworthy, and full of heart — in each and every token.

GIDDY represents what you put in, a near 1:1 swap for your dollar — no speculation, no surprises, unlocking new ways to spend and gift! Anchored in single-sided liquidity, it behaves like a stablecoin but remains free from centralized control. It’s the foundation for building #MicroEconomies — small, resilient loops where value flows between businesses, creators and communities.

GIDDY paves the way for organic growth through new ways to spend, building and exchanging real-world value through products at Giddys.Shop — pay with BOUNCY BALL for lower prices and receive digital gifts in GIDDY. Every purchase fuels growth, with thousands already trading BOUNCY BALL in support of our mission — now you can seamlessly swap between a dynamic token and lock in the profit with GIDDY. You earn just by shopping — no cost, no catch. The groundwork is set. Now we show what’s next! 🚀
