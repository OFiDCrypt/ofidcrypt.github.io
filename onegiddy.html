What is ONE GIDDY StableToken?

Canada’s first (unofficial) stable digital asset, designed to reflect the value of 1 Canadian Dollar through real-time commerce. It captures the spirit of our magical character Giddy — playful, trustworthy, and full of heart — in each and every token.

GIDDY paves the way for organic growth through new ways to spend, building and exchanging real-world value through products at Giddys Shop — pay with BOUNCY BALL for lower prices on store goods and receive cash back in digital gifts; offering savings to keep, grow or spend on your next purchase!

Every sale fuels growth, with thousands already trading BOUNCY BALL in support of our mission — now you can seamlessly swap between a dynamic token and lock in the profit with GIDDY. You earn just by shopping — no cost, no catch! 🚀
