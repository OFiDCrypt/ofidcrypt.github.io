We are a registered Canadian Web3 Family-First Brand dedicated to giving value back to consumers on each and every store purchase through joyful toys and digital gifts.

Giddy's brings joyful family adventures through lovable characters and toys that secretly unlock real digital value.

Our mission is to introduce new money in a fun way — every Giddy's toy comes with special Digital Gifts1 that grow over time. These new digital money tokens are featured in partner apps, where families can share, connect, chat, join groups and communities, and send, spend, and grow value together. The digital gifts are delivered straight to your wallet — the safe place where you chat with friends.

Giddy, our cheerful leading character, bounces through every story with her signature Bouncy Ball. Just like the legendary 1965 Wham-O Super Ball that bounced into millions of homes and sparked endless joy, Giddy's Bouncy Ball brings back that unstoppable fun — now paired with real digital gifts that keep growing.

At the heart of it all, our two tokens form what we call The Perfect Loop2 — the dynamic e𝕏PB Bouncy Ball3 for growth and excitement, paired with the stable GIDDY Coin4 that keeps everything grounded and reliable.

Together they power a fresh kind of loyalty where shopping with Giddy's delivers real discounts, savings, and growth — all while building #MicroEconomies5, simple positive loops where families and brands win in a new digital world.
